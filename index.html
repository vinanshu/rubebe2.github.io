About

Welcome to RuBEBE's Donut, where every bite is a blissful journey into the realm of sweetness and delight. Established with a passion for crafting delectable treats that ignite joy, RuBEBE's Donut has been a beloved fixture in our community since MARCH 23, 2024. Nestled in the heart of Villanueva Misamis Oriental, our quaint storefront beckons locals and visitors alike with the irresistible aroma of freshly baked donuts.
